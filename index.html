Who are We?

E-waste Kenya is a company that links, individuals who wish to dispose of their Electronic waste responsibly with the proper recycling and dismantling companies in Kenya to ensure sustainable production and consumption of E-Waste in alignment to the UN SDG Goal 12. What is E-waste?

E-waste is the short form of Electronic waste, often used to describe used electronics that are nearing the end of their useful life, and are discarded, donated or given to a recycler.Globally, e-waste constitutes more than 5 percent of all municipal solid waste and is increasing with the rise of sales of electronic products in developing countries.
